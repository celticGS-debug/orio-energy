# Orio Energy — Design Brainstorm

## Brief Summary
High-converting lead generation landing page for Orio Electrical Services Ltd. Solar & battery installer. Target: West Sussex homeowners 45-65. One goal: form submission. Brand: premium, trustworthy, local tradesperson.

---

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement**: Brutalist Editorial — heavy type, raw structure, unapologetic contrast
**Core Principles**:
1. Typography as architecture — headlines dominate, text is the layout
2. Hard edges, no softening — trust comes from directness not polish
3. Monochromatic with one violent accent
4. Information hierarchy through scale alone

**Color Philosophy**: Near-black navy (#0F2340) as canvas, raw white, single electric teal punch. No gradients. No softness.
**Layout Paradigm**: Full-bleed text blocks, oversized numbers, stacked columns with deliberate misalignment
**Signature Elements**: Oversized rule lines between sections, massive stat numbers, raw form with thick borders
**Interaction Philosophy**: Instant, no-nonsense — hover states are abrupt, not smooth
**Animation**: Snap-in, no easing. Numbers slam into place.
**Typography System**: Fraunces 900 italic at 96px+ for headlines, DM Sans 400 for body, monospace for stats
</idea>

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement**: Refined Craftsman — premium tradesperson aesthetic, warm materials, earned trust
**Core Principles**:
1. Warmth within darkness — navy as depth, not coldness
2. Every element earns its place — no decoration for decoration's sake
3. Human at the centre — Oliver's presence anchors every trust signal
4. Conversion through calm confidence, not urgency theatre

**Color Philosophy**: Deep navy (#0F2340 → #1B3A5C) as the foundation of authority. Teal (#00A79D) used sparingly as the action colour — it signals "go" without screaming. Warm white (#F8F5F0) for breathing sections. The palette reads as premium British tradesperson, not tech startup.
**Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric split layouts on desktop (60/40 or 55/45). Content-left, visual-right in hero. Generous vertical rhythm. Sections alternate between dark navy and warm white to create breathing room and visual interest.
**Signature Elements**: 
1. Fraunces italic teal for emotional emphasis within headlines — creates a visual rhythm
2. Floating caption cards with subtle shadows for Oliver's photo
3. Trust pill badges — small, rounded, grouped — as a recurring motif
**Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth, confident. Hover states feel like a firm handshake — responsive but not eager. Form button has a slow pulse that says "ready when you are."
**Animation**: Staggered fade-up on load (0.1s delays). Scroll-triggered fade-up for sections. Number count-up for stats. Spring-physics on form success tick. Nothing gratuitous.
**Typography System**: Fraunces (serif, variable weight, italic for emphasis) for all H1/H2. DM Sans (clean, modern) for body, labels, UI. Size scale: H1 56-72px, H2 40-48px, body 16-18px. Line height 1.4 for headlines, 1.6 for body.
</idea>

**Animation**: Slow fade-in (600ms), parallax on hero image, number count-up with ease-out

## Selected Approach: **Refined Craftsman**

Deep navy foundation with teal accents. Asymmetric split layouts. Fraunces serif italic for emotional emphasis. Warm white alternating sections. Oliver as the human anchor. Conversion through calm confidence.
